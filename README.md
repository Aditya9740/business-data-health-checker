# 🚀 Business Data Health Checker (Coming Soon)

## 📌 What is this?

A simple tool that helps businesses upload their raw data (Excel/CSV) and instantly get:

* Data quality issues (missing values, duplicates)
* Business insights (revenue leaks, slow sales cycles)
* Actionable recommendations

---

## 💡 Why I am building this

Most businesses have data, but they don’t know:

* where they are losing money
* why deals are getting delayed
* which areas need improvement

This tool solves that.

---

## ⚙️ Planned Features

* Upload CSV/Excel
* Automated data cleaning (Python)
* Insight generation
* KPI summary
* Downloadable report
* Dashboard view
